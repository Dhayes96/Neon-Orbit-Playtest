Tap in Time

Neon rings shrink toward the center circle. Tap the moment a ring lands on it — timing is everything.

How to Play

◎Neon rings shrink toward the glowing target circle. Tap in time the moment a ring lands on it. On Stage 1 you can tap anywhere — just learn the rhythm.
◉Tap Mode (in Settings): Target (recommended) requires tapping inside the dashed zone in time. Anywhere is timing-only for casual/accessible play. Space/Enter are always timing-only.
➤From Stage 2 the target moves — kept in a thumb-friendly zone on phones. A ghost circle shows where it's headed; follow it with your eyes, not your thumb.
♫The soundtrack evolves every five stages and the background shifts with it. Pick a Music Vibe in Settings — six distinct songs (Cyber Bounce, Blue Pulse, Toxic Breaks, and more). Visual theme and vibe can be set separately.
✦Perfect = 300 pts & refills energy. Good = 150. OK = 75. Late or early taps drain energy.
⚡Build combo to chase milestones: 25× Combo Fever (×2 score), 50× Overdrive, 75× Supernova, 100× Perfect Orbit — each pays a bonus. Long rhythm runs show up more from Stage 5.
▲Each new level gives a brief breather: gameplay pauses, the level and track are announced, then a fresh pattern begins.
♥Your energy bar drains over time and on misses. When it empties, the run ends.
☣Boss Battles at Stages 20, 30, 40 & 50. Damage the boss by hitting rings — Perfect hits hurt most, Good chips in, OK doesn't. Survive its phases to win energy. Progression pauses until the boss falls.
✦Defeat a boss to capture its core — Hex Saw, Tri-Laser, Prism Guard, or Eclipse Realm. Equip one in Settings, then hold the target (or hold Space) for half a second to unleash it. Rings your core destroys count as PERFECT, and energy drain pauses while it's running.
★Runs earn XP. Level up to unlock neon themes and Music Vibes in Settings.
🏆Your Highest Score across all runs is shown on the home screen. Chase your personal best — a leaderboard is coming later.

The Signal

I · The Grid

Between the stars hangs a lattice of light called the Grid, a woven barrier stretched over a thousand worlds. Beneath it, millions of species wake, build, argue, love and sleep without ever knowing it is there.

That is the point. A shield everyone can see is a shield someone will test.

II · The Signal

The Grid is not powered. It is played. Every world it shelters sits inside one enormous standing wave, and that wave keeps time to a single pure pulse the old engineers named the Signal.

While the Signal holds its tempo, the lattice holds. Let the beat thin, and the light thins with it. Let it stop, and the barrier simply opens.

III · What Waits Outside

They come from worlds that already went dark. Vast, patient intelligences that outlived their own suns and decided nothing else deserved to keep burning.

They have thrown fleets at the Grid. They have thrown centuries at it. None of it worked, because you cannot shatter a wave. You can only pull it out of step.

So they stopped attacking the barrier and started attacking the beat. They built wardens: machine-minds the size of moons, each one made to crowd the Signal, flood it with noise, and break its rhythm.

IV · Why Music

A signal that only defends decays. It has to be fed, and only one thing feeds it: the substance it is made of. Sound with intention. Music.

Every clean strike pushes light back into the lattice. Every one you let slip takes some out. Play well enough for long enough and the Grid does more than hold. It grows brighter, and the things outside feel it.

V · You

There is no fleet coming. No relay, no backup, no second Signal waiting to take over.

You are the Signal. Hold the beat. Keep the Grid alive.
