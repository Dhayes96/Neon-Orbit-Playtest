How to Play

◎Neon rings shrink toward the glowing target circle. Tap in time the moment a ring lands on it. On Stage 1 you can tap anywhere — just learn the rhythm.
◉From Stage 3 a tap zone appears (the dashed ring). Your tap must land inside it. On time but outside = OFF TARGET — it breaks combo but costs less than a miss. Space/Enter always count as timing-only.
➤From Stage 2 the target moves. A ghost circle shows where it's headed — follow it with your eyes, not your thumb.
♫Rhythms change as you climb — steady beats early, then syncopation, bursts, and rests. Tempo and intensity rise every level.
✦Perfect = 300 pts & refills energy. Good = 150. OK = 75. Late or early taps drain energy.
⚡Build combo to chase milestones: 25× Combo Fever (×2 score), 50× Overdrive, 75× Supernova, 100× Perfect Orbit — each pays a bonus.
▲Each new level gives a brief breather: gameplay pauses, the level and track are announced, then a fresh pattern begins.
♥Your energy bar drains over time and on misses. When it empties, the run ends.
★Runs earn XP. Level up to unlock neon themes in Settings.
📅Today's Challenge gives everyone the same ring pattern for the day. Set your daily best.
