How to Play

◎Neon rings shrink toward the glowing target circle. Tap in time the moment a ring lands on it. On Stage 1 you can tap anywhere — just learn the rhythm.
◉Tap Mode (in Settings): Hybrid (recommended) taps anywhere early, then inside the dashed zone from Stage 3. Target Zone requires the zone from Stage 2. Anywhere is timing-only for casual/accessible play. Space/Enter are always timing-only.
➤From Stage 2 the target moves — kept in a thumb-friendly zone on phones. A ghost circle shows where it's headed; follow it with your eyes, not your thumb.
♫The soundtrack evolves every five stages and the background shifts with it. Pick a Music Vibe in Settings — six distinct songs (Cyber Bounce, Blue Pulse, Toxic Breaks, and more). Visual theme and vibe can be set separately.
✦Perfect = 300 pts & refills energy. Good = 150. OK = 75. Late or early taps drain energy.
⚡Build combo to chase milestones: 25× Combo Fever (×2 score), 50× Overdrive, 75× Supernova, 100× Perfect Orbit — each pays a bonus. Long rhythm runs show up more from Stage 5.
▲Each new level gives a brief breather: gameplay pauses, the level and track are announced, then a fresh pattern begins.
♥Your energy bar drains over time and on misses. When it empties, the run ends.
☣Boss Battles every 25 stages (25/50/75/100). Damage the boss by hitting rings — Perfect hits hurt most, Good chips in, OK doesn't. Survive its phases to win energy. Progression pauses until the boss falls.
✦Defeat each boss once to unlock an Ability — Orbit Burst, Pulse Guard, Precision Surge, or Eclipse Rebirth. Equip one in Settings; the active ones get a button in-game.
★Runs earn XP. Level up to unlock neon themes and Music Vibes in Settings.
📅Today's Challenge gives everyone the same ring pattern for the day. Set your daily best.
